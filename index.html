How It Works

Collect. Decide. Respond. Correlate. Analyze. Map. Draft. Decide. Comply. Respond. Isolate. Report. Collect. Decide. Respond.

HemisX ingests AWS CloudTrail, VPC Flow Logs, and GuardDuty findings via Kinesis in under 5 seconds. Every event is normalized and fed into a live threat graph — your real-time security nervous system. The AI engine maps every event across services and identities, correlating lateral movement chains and privilege escalation patterns. Real attacks surface from noise — automatically, continuously. The LLM copilot drafts verdicts, playbooks, and compliance narratives with full source evidence attached. Every decision is human-in-the-loop — you approve, refine, or override before action. Auto-run playbooks isolate affected assets, revoke IAM credentials, and generate compliance-ready reports in minutes. Mean time to respond drops by 70% — from hours to single-digit minutes. From log ingestion to automated response, HemisX's AI pipeline collects, correlates, decides, and acts — cutting mean time to respond by 70%.
